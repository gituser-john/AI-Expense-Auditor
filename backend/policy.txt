Acme Corp - Corporate Travel and Expense Policy

1. General Guidelines
All employees must ensure corporate funds are used exclusively for legitimate business purposes. Itemized receipts are strictly required for any individual expense exceeding $25.00.

2. Meal Allowances
Employees traveling on official company business will be reimbursed for personal meals up to the following maximum limits (including tax and tip):

Breakfast: $20.00

Lunch: $30.00

Dinner: $50.00

Note: Meal limits are strictly per person. If expensing a meal for a group or clients, the business purpose must explicitly state the number of attendees, and the total must not exceed the per-person limit multiplied by the number of attendees.

3. Alcohol Restrictions
The purchase of alcoholic beverages is strictly prohibited for standard employee travel and will not be reimbursed under any circumstances unless pre-approved for a specific client entertainment event.

4. Transportation
Rideshare services (Uber/Lyft) and taxis are permitted for business-related travel. Standard or Economy tiers must be used. Luxury transportation tiers (e.g., Uber Black, Limousines) are expressly non-reimbursable.

5. Prohibited Expenses
The following items are non-reimbursable: personal care items, hotel minibar charges, in-room movies, entertainment (e.g., golf, theater tickets), and traffic/parking violations.
